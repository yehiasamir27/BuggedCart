What is Sensitive Data Exposure?

Occurs when applications don't properly protect sensitive data such as financial, healthcare, or PII.

Common Examples

  • Unencrypted storage of sensitive data
  • Inadequate key management
  • Transmitting data over unencrypted channels
  • Exposing sensitive data in URLs or logs

Mitigation (recommended)

  • Encrypt sensitive data at rest and in transit
  • Don't store unnecessary sensitive data
  • Use strong encryption algorithms
  • Do not reveal secrets in templates or responses
  • Implement proper access controls
